## Why the Windows XP theme?

I wanted to recapture some of the whimsy the old Web 1.0-style websites have, and there wasn't a better homage I could pay than to give it a faithful recreation. Plus it really works well with the SCSS limitations and static-page-only restrictions of GitHub Pages.
