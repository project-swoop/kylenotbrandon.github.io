Well...it all started after yesterday's run. I hit my 5K PR and in the euphoria and bliss of the runner's high, I thought to myself quite a familiar thought. 

*"Why don't I write a blog?"*

Truth be told, I was daunted - and frankly, too cheap to even think about it. I don't want to pay for WordPress. I don't want to pay for Squarespace. I don't want to pay for Wix. 

So I asked a friend (Google). *"How can I host a blog site for as cheap as possible?"*

Enter GitHub Pages. 

You guys, literally the only thing I had to do was buy my own domain. And even that was optional!

$3 Namecheap domain (and it's pretty sick, too). Free CloudFlare DNS. And GitHub just serves it as a repo.

It. Does. Not. Get. Better. Than. That. 

I feel like a bit of an internet pioneer. Is this 2002? People just making their own static Internet blogs for cheap? (I wouldn't know, I was born in 2002)

But in any case...this is now my blog, I guess. 

## What will we talk about?

Whatever's on my mind. Could be shallow, could be deep, could be somewhere in between. This is my stream of consciousness I can bombard you with. Sorry.

## How often will you post?

No more than daily, no less than whenever I feel like it.

## You're afraid of commitment.

I've heard that one before. 

---

In all seriousness, I'm super excited to turn this space into a (hopefully) judgment-free space which I can use to talk about what's on my mind - both most immediately and in saying some (maybe) deep things. We'll see, and time will tell.

In the meantime, hit me up at kylenotbrandon@gmail.com if there's anything you'd like to hear me opine on.
